The company has a team of about 200 employees, with manufacturing plant, technological center and commercial office. The company is building a safe and secure workplace where employees feel valued, where their rights are respected, and where they can do meaningful work.

Wedrone is designing and manufacturing products through the responsible use of raw materials and natural resources. The company is working to reduce its waste generation throughout the companies' life cycle.

By investing in research and development to improve efficiency, helping reducing the complexity of electric vehicles, Wedrone is contributing to combating climate change and its impact. Furthermore, Wedrone is working to reduce its CO2 emissions associated with its manufacturing processes.

Wedrone is working to ensure that conflict-free minerals are used in the manufacture of its automotive components. Wedrone will implement an annual supplier inquiry to collect the Standard Survey (EICC/Gesi template) on conflict minerals from all its suppliers.
